With the fixed LCB value, the system looks much more robust to safety-value noise. Even as noise increases from std=0.03 to std=0.06, the completion rate stays relatively high, conflicts remain at 0%, and the minimum-distance margins stay close to or above the no-noise baseline. The fixed LCB appears to make the safety filter more conservative: it reduces multiple simultaneous safety engagements and preserves larger worst-case minimum distances, but with a small cost in efficiency, since travel time and travel distance generally increase slightly compared to the no-noise case.
